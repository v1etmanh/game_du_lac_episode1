# Ke hoach cai thien toan bo minigame Du Lac Diary

## 1. Muc tieu chung

Ban ke hoach nay tap trung vao viec nang chat luong cam giac choi, them co che co chieu sau, va bien tung minigame thanh mot trai nghiem co ban sac rieng thay vi chi la mot man choi tach roi.

Muc tieu uu tien:

- Moi game phai co "hanh dong chinh" that da tay: bam phim, keo, tha, chon, hoi, hoac ra quyet dinh deu phai co phan hoi ro.
- Moi game can co mot vong lap 15-30 giay lap lai vui: nhin tinh huong, quyet dinh, thao tac, nhan phan hoi, duoc thuong hoac bi sua sai.
- Moi game can co co che tien bo: nguoi choi thay minh gioi hon sau 3 phut, khong chi thay diem tang.
- Moi game can gan voi chu de van hoa trong dia diem, nhung khong bien thanh bai thuyet minh dai.
- Moi game can co ket qua sau man choi de dong gop vao nhat ky, tien trinh ban do, hoac noi dung hoi thoai sau do.

## 2. Chuan cam giac choi cho tat ca game

### 2.1. Phan hoi dieu khien

- Moi input quan trong phai co phan hoi trong duoi 100 ms: am thanh ngan, hieu ung nho, animation, rung nhe tren UI, hoac thay doi trang thai nhan vat.
- Neu input khong hop le, game phai noi ly do bang hieu ung: nut rung, vung cam sang do, tooltip ngan, hoac am thanh "khong duoc".
- Han che cac thao tac chi co tac dung ngam. Nguoi choi phai nhin thay he qua cua hanh dong.

### 2.2. Onboarding trong game

- Moi game co 1 man tap sieu ngan, toi da 20 giay.
- Huong dan xuat hien theo ngu canh, khong dat mot bang chu dai truoc khi choi.
- Sau lan choi dau, game chi hien goi y nho neu nguoi choi bi ket hoac that bai lap lai.

### 2.3. Do kho va nhip do

- 30 giay dau: an toan, de hieu, co thanh cong nho.
- 30-90 giay: them bien so thu hai, bat dau can quyet dinh.
- Sau 90 giay: them ap luc nhung khong lam roi man hinh.
- Moi game can co "comeback window": nguoi choi thua nhe van co cach lat lai, khong bi ket cuc som.

### 2.4. Ket qua sau man choi

Moi game nen tra ve cac thong tin chung:

- Diem tong.
- Hang danh gia: S, A, B, C.
- 1 thanh tich noi bat: combo dai nhat, cau hoi hay nhat, cu bat dep nhat, nuoc di hay nhat.
- 1 kien thuc van hoa ngan duoi 2 cau.
- 1 vat pham/ghi chu them vao nhat ky.

## 3. Lo trinh uu tien

### Giai doan 1: Sua cam giac cot loi

Uu tien nhung game nguoi choi cham vao nhieu nhat:

1. Bat ga: sua thanh game dieu huong dan ga co chien thuat, khong chi duoi bat.
2. Hai qua: them combo, do chin, vat can va quyet dinh rung cay.
3. Tha dieu: doi truc gameplay de nguoi choi phu thuoc vao dieu va day dieu.
4. O an quan: lam nhanh, vui, co bat ngo, co nhan vat doi thu.
5. Phong su: tao bo luat hoi dap, bang chung, do tin cay va cham diem cau hoi.

### Giai doan 2: Dong bo tien trinh

- Moi game co man ket qua cung phong cach.
- Moi game unlock mot manh nhat ky hoac ky uc.
- Ban do hien dau an hoan thanh theo chat luong, khong chi hoan thanh/chua hoan thanh.

### Giai doan 3: Tang gia tri choi lai

- Them thu thach phu moi ngay hoac moi lan vao game.
- Them bien the do kho.
- Them thanh tich rieng cho tung game.
- Them cau hoi va tinh huong sinh ngau nhien co kiem soat.

## 4. Game Bat ga

### 4.1. Van de can giai quyet

Game hien co nen tranh cam giac "chay cham theo ga roi cho ga vao chuong". Neu nguoi choi chi bam di chuyen va doi ga, trai nghiem se nhanh lap lai. Can bien no thanh game doc hanh vi, dieu huong dam ga, dung thoc va tieng vo tay dung luc.

### 4.2. Trai nghiem muc tieu

Nguoi choi phai cam thay minh la nguoi dang lua dan ga vao chuong bang su binh tinh va tinh toan. Thanh cong den tu viec doc huong chay, dat moi, chan duong, mo cong dung thoi diem.

### 4.3. Co che moi

#### Trang thai tam ly cua ga

Moi con ga co 4 chi so:

- Binh tinh: de bi dan bang thoc.
- Hoang: chay nhanh nhung kho doan.
- Tin nguoi: de lai gan neu nguoi choi di cham.
- Doi: uu tien thoc hon viec chay tron.

Ga khong nen phan ung giong nhau. Can co cac kieu:

- Ga nhat: thay nguoi la chay, nhung de bi dan bang thoc.
- Ga tham an: bi hut manh boi thoc, nhung an lau.
- Ga dau dan: khi no chay, cac con gan do cung chay theo.
- Ga rong: kho vao chuong, tao ap luc cuoi man.

#### Co che tu the nguoi choi

Them 3 cach tiep can:

- Di thuong: toc do vua, ga canh giac trung binh.
- Di cham: giu phim de giam toc, ga it hoang hon, phu hop dan ga.
- Chay nuoc rut: nhanh nhung lam ga hoang va co the pha hong dan ga.

#### Thoc thanh duong dan

Thay vi tha tung bai thoc rieng le, cho phep tao "vet thoc":

- Giu phim de rai thoc thanh duong ngan.
- Ga an theo duong thoc neu khong bi hoang.
- Duong thoc co gioi han, bat nguoi choi quyet dinh noi dat.

#### Vo tay co huong

Vo tay khong chi la song tron:

- Bam nhanh: vo tay nho, day ga ra xa mot chut.
- Giu va tha: vo tay manh, tam rong hon nhung cooldown lau.
- Huong vo tay dua theo vi tri nhan vat, day ga ra khoi nguoi choi.

#### Cong chuong co rui ro

- Cong mo qua lau thi ga da vao co the di ra.
- Dong cong qua som thi chan mat duong dan.
- Can co "vung an toan" trong chuong: ga phai o yen 2-3 giay moi tinh la da vao chuong.

### 4.4. Nhip man choi de xuat

1. 0-30 giay: dan 2 ga de bang thoc.
2. 30-90 giay: them ga nhat va vat can.
3. 90-150 giay: ga dau dan anh huong ca dan.
4. Cuoi man: dong cong dung thoi diem de giu thanh qua.

### 4.5. Tieu chi nghiem thu

- Nguoi choi co the thang bang it nhat 2 chien thuat: dung thoc nhieu hoac dung vo tay chan duong.
- Mot lan choi tot nhin ro khac mot lan choi xau, khong chi khac ve thoi gian.
- Ga khong bi ket goc, khong rung lap lien tuc.
- That bai phai co ly do doc duoc: lam ga hoang, mo cong sai luc, dat thoc sai vi tri.

## 5. Game Hai qua

### 5.1. Van de can giai quyet

Game hai qua can them chieu sau ngoai viec chay den cho qua roi nhat. Nen tao cam giac "vuon cay song dong": qua co do chin, cay co suc, nguoi choi phai quyet dinh rung cay, bat qua, phan loai va giu combo.

### 5.2. Co che moi

#### Do chin cua qua

Qua co 3 trang thai:

- Xanh: roi cham, diem thap, co the lam mat combo neu bat qua nhieu.
- Chin vua: diem chuan, de bat.
- Chin vang: diem cao, roi nhanh, thoi gian ton tai ngan.

Nguoi choi khong chi bat tat ca, ma phai uu tien qua tot.

#### Combo bat qua

- Bat lien tiep khong de qua roi: tang combo.
- Bat dung loai qua dang duoc yeu cau: tang nhan diem.
- Nhay bat qua o sat mat dat: "cuu qua", them diem ky nang.

#### Rung cay co rui ro

Nut rung cay khong chi lam roi nhanh:

- Rung nhe: roi 1-2 qua, an toan.
- Rung manh: roi nhieu qua, nhung co the lam roi qua xanh hoac lam cay met.
- Cay met se giam toc sinh qua trong thoi gian ngan.

#### Gio va quy dao roi

- Gio lam qua lech trai/phai.
- La cay va hat bui bao truoc huong gio.
- Nguoi choi gioi co the dung gio de bat chuoi combo.

#### Gio hang yeu cau

Moi vong co "don hang":

- 3 xoai chin.
- 2 oi va 1 man.
- Chi tinh qua chin vang.

Don hang tao muc tieu ngan, giup game bot thanh bai toan thu thap so luong lon.

#### Vat can va dia hinh

- Luong rau / luong cay thap: can nhay qua.
- Vung bun: chay cham nhung co nhieu qua hiem.
- Gio qua: co the dat tam thoi lam diem gom, nhung phai quay lai lay.

### 5.3. Cai thien cam giac

- Khi bat qua, qua nen hut nhe vao tay trong 0.1 giay cuoi de thao tac mem hon.
- Khi combo cao, am thanh bat qua tang cao do.
- Khi qua sap cham dat, them vong canh bao nho tren mat dat.
- Khi rung cay, than cay rung va la roi de nguoi choi thay luc.

### 5.4. Tieu chi nghiem thu

- Sau 60 giay, nguoi choi phai co it nhat 3 quyet dinh khac nhau: bat qua nao, rung cay nao, bo qua qua nao.
- Combo phai khuyen khich choi gioi nhung khong lam nguoi moi that vong.
- Man choi khong nen yeu cau nhat 200 qua thuan tuy neu khong co moc trung gian.

## 6. Game Tha dieu

### 6.1. Van de can giai quyet

Game tha dieu can doi co che de nguoi choi phu thuoc nhieu hon vao dieu. Neu nhan vat co the vuot chuong ngai chu yeu bang chay/nhay, con dieu chi la ho tro, thi chu de bi yeu. Nen bien dieu, day dieu va gio thanh trung tam cua moi quyet dinh.

### 6.2. Trai nghiem muc tieu

Nguoi choi khong "dieu khien nhan vat co mang theo dieu", ma "dieu khien moi quan he giua nguoi, day, gio va dieu". Dieu keo nguoi choi, nang nguoi choi, lam mat thang bang, va mo duong qua canh dong.

### 6.3. Co che thay doi cot loi

#### Day dieu la nguon luc chinh

Day co 3 trang thai:

- Chung: dieu mat luc nang, nguoi choi cham lai.
- Cang vua: dieu on dinh, cho phep tang toc va nhay xa.
- Qua cang: nguy co dut day hoac bi keo ve phia gio.

Nguoi choi phai giu day trong vung tot bang cach tha/thu day va doi vi tri.

#### Dieu keo huong di chuyen

- Gio trai/phai lam dieu keo nguoi choi theo huong do.
- Thu day de tang luc keo trong thoi gian ngan.
- Tha day de giam luc keo, tranh va cham, hoac cho dieu bay cao hon.

#### Nhay phu thuoc vao luc nang cua dieu

- Nhan vat khong nen co cu nhay manh co dinh.
- Neu dieu dang co luc nang, nhan vat moi nhay xa/luot qua chuong ngai.
- Neu day chung hoac dieu thap, nhay yeu va de roi.

#### Muc tieu nam tren duong bay

Them cac vong gio, not nhac dieu sao, hoac dai lua tren khong:

- Dua dieu qua vong gio de lay diem.
- Giu dieu o vung gio vang de nap luc nang.
- Tranh chim muon/chuong ngai tren cao bang cach doi do cao.

#### Tai nan co the cuu

- Neu dieu sap roi, nguoi choi co 2 giay de chay nguoc gio va keo lai.
- Neu day qua cang, nguoi choi co the tha day dung luc de tranh dut.
- Loi nho co the sua, tao cam giac ky nang thay vi thua dot ngot.

### 6.4. Bo dieu khien de xuat

- Trai/phai: di chuyen nhan vat.
- Len/xuong hoac W/S: thu/tha day.
- Space: bat nhip nhay, do manh dua vao luc nang hien tai.
- Shift: ghim chan trong thoi gian ngan de dieu tang do cang, dung cho cac pha keo manh.

### 6.5. Tieu chi nghiem thu

- Neu khong quan tam den dieu, nguoi choi phai choi te hon ro ret.
- Mot pha vuot vat can dep phai can dung thoi diem cang day, huong gio va nhay.
- UI phai hien ro trang thai day: chung, tot, nguy hiem.
- Game phai khuyen khich nhin len dieu, khong chi nhin duong chay duoi dat.

## 7. Game O an quan

### 7.1. Van de can giai quyet

O an quan co luat hay nhung de bi cham neu chi bam o va xem so quan doi. Muc tieu la giu tinh dan gian nhung lam trai nghiem "sieu giai tri": nhanh, bat ngo, da mat, co doi thu co tinh cach, va giup nguoi choi cam thay moi nuoc di deu co kich tinh.

### 7.2. Che do choi

#### Che do co dien

- Giu luat chuan.
- Giai thich bang animation rai quan va highlight o an.
- Phu hop cho nguoi moi.

#### Che do le hoi

Them bien the vui sau khi nguoi choi da hieu luat:

- Nuoc di vang: moi 3 luot xuat hien 1 o co nhan diem neu rai qua.
- Tran gio: gioi han 12 giay moi luot, tao ap luc vui.
- Quan may man: mot vien soi dac biet nhan doi diem neu bi an.
- Cuoc nho: truoc van, nguoi choi chon muc rui ro de tang thuong.

### 7.3. Lam game vui hon

#### Animation co nhip

- Rai quan tung vien co am thanh "cach" ngan.
- Toc do animation tang dan neu nguoi choi da xem nhieu lan.
- Khi an duoc nhieu quan, camera/board rung nhe va diem bay ve phia nguoi choi.

#### Du doan nuoc di

- Khi hover/chon o, hien duong rai quan.
- Hien du kien: "co the an", "se rai tiep", "rui ro cao".
- Khong hien dap an toi uu ngay, chi goi y muc do.

#### Doi thu co tinh cach

Moi NPC co style:

- Ong ba: di chac, uu tien giu quan.
- Hung: lieu, hay chon nuoc an lon.
- Ba Nam: hay giang bay, thich buoc nguoi choi het dan.

Doi thu nen co cau noi ngan khi co nuoc hay, nhung khong spam.

#### Khoanh khac kich tinh

- Khi sap an quan lon, game lam cham 0.4 giay.
- Khi nguoi choi bi doi thu dao nguoc tinh the, hien "lat keo".
- Khi con 2 quan cuoi, them nhac nen cang hon.

### 7.4. Tieu chi nghiem thu

- Mot luot co the hieu bang mat, khong can doc log.
- Van co dien khong qua dai: muc tieu 3-6 phut.
- Che do le hoi tao tieng cuoi va bat ngo nhung khong pha hoan toan luat goc.
- AI khong nen luon toi uu. Doi thu can co ca sai lam co tinh cach.

## 8. Game Phong su

### 8.1. Van de can giai quyet

Phong su khong nen chi la chat voi NPC. Can co bo luat dieu tra, bang chung, do tin cay, cam xuc, mau thuan va ky nang dat cau hoi. Nguoi choi phai cam thay minh nhu mot nha bao tre: quan sat, dat cau hoi hay, khai thac thong tin tinh te, kiem chung va viet bai co trach nhiem.

### 8.2. Nguyen tac thiet ke

- Moi cau hoi phai co muc dich: mo chu de, dao sau, kiem chung, doi chieu, hay lam ro cam xuc.
- NPC khong nen noi het neu bi hoi chung chung.
- Thong tin co do tin cay, nguon goc va muc do nhay cam.
- Nguoi choi co the dat cau hoi te, va game phai phan hoi bang viec NPC ne tranh, mat tin tuong, hoac tra loi ngan.
- Muc tieu khong phai "hoi du so cau", ma la lap duoc buc tranh dung va co bang chung.

### 8.3. Bo chi so phong van

Moi cuoc phong van co 5 chi so:

- Tin tuong: NPC co san sang noi that hay khong.
- Do mo chu de: nguoi choi da khai pha bao nhieu mang thong tin.
- Do sau: moi chu de co du chi tiet, vi du, cam xuc, nguyen nhan hay chua.
- Do kiem chung: thong tin quan trong da co bang chung hoac nguon thu hai chua.
- Dao duc nghe bao: nguoi choi co ton trong nhan vat, khong ep cung, khong bop meo loi noi hay khong.

### 8.4. Cau truc thong tin

Moi NPC can co "ho so thong tin" gom:

- Su kien cot loi: dieu bat buoc de hieu cau chuyen.
- Ky uc ca nhan: mau sac cam xuc, giup bai viet co hon.
- Chi tiet an: chi lo ra khi hoi dung huong.
- Mau thuan: dieu khac voi loi ke cua NPC khac hoac voi vat chung.
- Ranh gioi: chu de NPC khong muon noi neu chua du tin tuong.
- Cau noi trich dan: cau hay co the dua vao bai bao.

### 8.5. Cac loai cau hoi

Nguoi choi nen duoc chon hoac tu tao cau hoi theo 8 loai:

1. Cau hoi mo: "Bac co the ke lai chuyen do tu dau khong?"
2. Cau hoi moc thoi gian: "Luc do xay ra truoc hay sau le hoi?"
3. Cau hoi cam xuc: "Khi nghe tieng trong luc ay, bac thay the nao?"
4. Cau hoi chi tiet vat the: "Chiec dan/quan co/vat nay luc do o dau?"
5. Cau hoi doi chieu: "Dieu nay co khac voi loi ke cua Hung khong?"
6. Cau hoi nguyen nhan: "Vi sao moi nguoi lai lam nhu vay?"
7. Cau hoi he qua: "Sau do lang minh thay doi dieu gi?"
8. Cau hoi xac nhan trich dan: "Chau co the ghi dung cau nay vao bai bao khong?"

### 8.6. Quy luat NPC tra loi

NPC khong nen tra loi theo danh sach co dinh. Can co quy luat:

- Cau hoi chung chung: tra loi chung, mo 1 manh moi nho.
- Cau hoi bam dung chi tiet: mo thong tin sau.
- Cau hoi lap lai: giam tin tuong hoac NPC nhac "chau vua hoi roi".
- Cau hoi gay ap luc qua som: NPC phong thu, khoa chu de nhay cam.
- Cau hoi co bang chung: tang kha nang NPC thua nhan chi tiet bi che giau.
- Cau hoi dong cam sau khi NPC xuc dong: tang tin tuong.

### 8.7. He thong bang chung

Nguoi choi co so tay gom 4 loai bang chung:

- Loi ke: cau tra loi cua NPC.
- Vat chung: anh, vat the, ban ve, nhac cu, quan co.
- Quan sat: dieu nguoi choi thay trong scene.
- Doi chieu: thong tin tu NPC khac.

Moi thong tin quan trong can gan nhan:

- Chua kiem chung.
- Da co 1 nguon.
- Da co 2 nguon.
- Mau thuan.
- Co the trich dan.

### 8.8. Cham diem cau hoi hay

Game nen cham "cau hoi hay" theo cac tieu chi:

- Dung ngu canh: hoi dung chu de dang mo.
- Cu the: nhac den nguoi, vat, thoi diem, dia diem ro.
- Mo duong: giup lo ra thong tin moi.
- Ton trong: khong quy chup, khong ep cung.
- Co follow-up: dua tren cau tra loi vua nhan.

Vi du cau hoi tot:

- "Luc ong noi 'hom do khac moi ngay', cu the co am thanh hay dau hieu nao lam ong nho nhat?"
- "Ba vua nhac den chiec dan bau. Ai la nguoi giu no truoc khi dem dien?"
- "Hung noi rang tro o an quan hom do bi dung lai giua chung. Bac co nho ly do khong?"

Vi du cau hoi kem:

- "Ke het di."
- "Co phai bac noi doi khong?"
- "Chuyen nay co gi hay?"

### 8.9. Luat viet bai bao

Sau phong van, nguoi choi viet bai theo yeu cau:

- Tieu de khong duoc sai su that.
- Mo bai phai neu duoc ai, o dau, chuyen gi.
- Than bai can co it nhat 2 bang chung.
- Neu co mau thuan, phai ghi la "theo loi ke" hoac "chua duoc xac nhan".
- Trich dan phai dung y, khong cat ghep lam sai nghia.
- Ket bai nen rut ra y nghia van hoa, khong chi tong ket su kien.

### 8.10. Tieu chi nghiem thu

- Nguoi choi gioi co the lay thong tin sau ma nguoi choi hoi loa khong lay duoc.
- NPC co phan ung khac nhau theo cach hoi.
- Bai bao cuoi cung co chat luong khac nhau dua tren bang chung, khong chi dua vao so cau hoi.
- He thong khuyen khich "hoi hay" thay vi spam tat ca lua chon.

## 9. Game Dan bau

### 9.1. Huong cai thien

Dan bau nen la game cam nhac co cam giac cham vao day dan, khong chi la bam dung not.

Co che de xuat:

- Tach diem thanh 3 phan: dung thoi diem, dung cao do, do muot khi vuot can dan.
- Them co che ngan/rung: giu not dung luc de tao vibrato.
- Them cac not luot: nguoi choi keo cao do qua mot vung thay vi bam diem don.
- Them do kho theo lop: de thi chi can timing, kho thi can pitch bend.
- Man ket qua hien "net choi": on dinh, bay bong, dung nhip, hay rung qua tay.

Cam giac can co:

- Not dung phai co am thanh trong, anh sang mem tren day dan.
- Not lech cao do phai nghe hoi chenh, nhung khong qua kho chiu.
- Can dan phai co inertia nhe, khong nhay tuc thoi nhu con tro chuot.

Tieu chi nghiem thu:

- Nguoi choi co the cam nhan minh dang dieu khien am sac dan bau.
- Diem cao khong chi den tu viec bam dung nhac, ma tu cach giu va luot not dep.

## 10. Game Ban hang

### 10.1. Huong cai thien

Ban hang co the thanh game quan sat khach, can do, mac ca va phuc vu nhanh.

Co che de xuat:

- Khach co tinh cach: de tinh, kho tinh, vo vang, thich mac ca.
- Moi mat hang co do tuoi/ngon, anh huong gia va su hai long.
- Can hang co sai so: nguoi choi can can dung, khong qua thieu hoac qua du.
- Mac ca co rui ro: gia cao hon thi loi hon nhung khach co the bo di.
- Don hang combo: khach mua nhieu mon, can sap xep thu tu phuc vu.
- Tin don cho: mot so mon tang gia trong thoi gian ngan.

Cam giac can co:

- Keo qua vao can phai co trong luong ro.
- Tien leng keng, khach phan ung bang net mat.
- Sai so can nang phai duoc hien bang kim can va am thanh.

Tieu chi nghiem thu:

- Nguoi choi phai ra quyet dinh giua nhanh, dung va loi.
- Khach khac nhau can cach phuc vu khac nhau.

## 11. Game Giai do / Nha cu

### 11.1. Huong cai thien

Nha cu dang ket hop giai do va hai qua. Nen bien thanh chuoi dieu tra nho: tim manh moi trong nha cu, giai cau do, roi dung ket qua de mo mot bien the hai qua hoac ky uc.

Co che de xuat:

- So tay manh moi: moi vat tim thay duoc luu lai.
- Cau do lien hoan: dap an cau truoc mo cach hieu cau sau.
- Vat the co the xoay/lat/mo, tao cam giac cham vao do vat cu.
- Goi y theo bac: goi y 1 chi huong nhin, goi y 2 chi vat the, goi y 3 gan dap an nhung tru diem.
- Mot so manh moi chi xuat hien neu nguoi choi doc ky hoi thoai truoc do.

Tieu chi nghiem thu:

- Cau do co logic noi tai, khong phai do meo.
- Nguoi choi co the giai bang manh moi trong game, khong can doan mo.
- Goi y giup het ket nhung khong cuop mat cam giac chien thang.

## 12. Tich hop voi ban do va nhat ky

### 12.1. He thong thuong chung

Moi game sau khi hoan thanh nen tra ve:

- 1 ky vat.
- 1 cau chuyen ngan.
- 1 thanh tich.
- 1 cap do hoan thanh.

Vi du:

- Bat ga: "nam thoc vang", ghi chu ve cach goi ga vao chuong.
- Hai qua: "gio qua chin", ghi chu ve mua vuon.
- Tha dieu: "doan day dieu cu", ghi chu ve gio dong.
- O an quan: "vien soi quan", ghi chu ve tro choi dan gian.
- Phong su: "the nha bao tap su", ghi chu ve nguon tin.
- Dan bau: "mong gay dan", ghi chu ve am sac dan bau.

### 12.2. Chat luong hoan thanh

Ban do nen hien:

- Hoan thanh C: da qua.
- Hoan thanh B: nam duoc co che.
- Hoan thanh A: choi tot.
- Hoan thanh S: co thanh tich dep hoac khai thac du thong tin.

Nguoi choi khong bi chan tien trinh neu chi dat C, nhung co ly do quay lai de dat A/S.

## 13. Checklist truoc khi trien khai

Truoc khi code tung game, can chot:

- Hanh dong chinh cua game la gi?
- Nguoi choi can nhin dau tren man hinh?
- Dieu gi tao cam giac da tay?
- That bai thuong gap la gi va game giai thich no the nao?
- Co che nao gan voi van hoa/dia diem?
- Mot man choi ly tuong dai bao lau?
- Ket qua nao duoc dua ve nhat ky?

## 14. Thu tu trien khai de xuat

1. Bat ga: vi co nen tang simulation san, chi can nang hanh vi va muc tieu.
2. Tha dieu: vi can doi truc cot loi som, tranh xay them tren co che chua dung.
3. Hai qua: them combo/don hang/do chin de tang vui nhanh.
4. Phong su: xay bo luat cau hoi va bang chung, day la he thong lon.
5. O an quan: them che do le hoi, animation va doi thu.
6. Dan bau: tinh lai pitch/timing va man ket qua.
7. Ban hang: them khach, mac ca, can nang.
8. Nha cu/giai do: ket noi manh moi voi tien trinh nhat ky.

## 15. Dinh nghia "xong" cho ban nang cap

Mot minigame duoc xem la nang cap dat yeu cau khi:

- Nguoi choi moi hieu cach choi trong 30 giay.
- Nguoi choi cu co it nhat 2 cach choi tot khac nhau.
- Man choi co phan hoi ro cho thanh cong, loi va gan thanh cong.
- Co it nhat 1 co che rieng khong game nao khac co.
- Ket qua sau man choi co y nghia voi hanh trinh Du Lac Diary.
